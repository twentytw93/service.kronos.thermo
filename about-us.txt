# About Kronos Team

**Kronos Team** is an independent collective dedicated to building safe, lightweight, and innovative add-ons for Kodi and LibreELEC. 
Our focus is on performance, reliability, and user empowerment — software built by the community, for the community. 

We design tools that extend Kodi beyond entertainment into stability, privacy, and system control, while keeping every add-on simple, clean, and compatible.
